What We Learned from This Research

What Makes EcoHub Special

After comparing our website with these 5 other websites, we found some things that make EcoHub different and special:

  • URL Parameter Checkout: Our website uses a simple way to pass product information to the checkout page through the URL. Other websites use more complicated shopping cart systems. Our method is simpler and works well for a static website.
  • Consistent Gradient Design: We use gradient colors everywhere on our website - in the navigation bar, section backgrounds, and buttons. This gives our website a modern and unique look. Some other websites use gradients, but not as much as we do.
  • Complete Product Information: Every product on our website has a detailed table showing specifications like size, material, and capacity. Some websites like Package Free and Pela Case don't show this information in tables.

Features That All Websites Have

We noticed that all successful eco-friendly websites share some common features:

  • Sticky navigation bar that stays at the top when you scroll down
  • Grid layout that arranges products in rows and columns
  • Hover effects that show animation when you move your mouse over items
  • Blog or educational content to teach people about sustainability
  • Responsive design that works on phones, tablets, and computers
  • Box shadows to make cards look like they are floating above the page

What Other Websites Do Well

Each website we researched has some special features that we can learn from:

  • Patagonia: They use a very simple, clean design. They don't show many discounts because they want customers to focus on product quality, not price. This works well for their brand.
  • The Body Shop: They provide very detailed information about ingredients and how to use products. This is helpful for customers who care about health.
  • EarthHero: They show environmental impact data for products, like how much carbon dioxide is saved. This is great for customers who want to see exact numbers.

Final Thoughts

This research helped us understand what makes a good e-commerce website. We learned that our EcoHub website has many features that are common in successful websites (like sticky navigation and grid layouts), but we also have some unique features (like URL parameter checkout and consistent gradient design). All the websites we looked at try to balance good design with useful features. They all have educational content because eco-friendly websites need to teach people, not just sell products. We are proud that EcoHub has its own style while still following best practices from successful websites.
